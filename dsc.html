✦ Our Story

Meet the Artist

5+
Years of
Excellence

Hello! I'm Basavakiran Biradar, the founder and lead photographer of Dreams Capture Studios, based in the heart of Basavakalyan, Karnataka.

My journey with photography began as a childhood fascination with light, shadow, and the stories that moments hold. Over the years, that passion transformed into a calling — to preserve life's most precious milestones through the art of photography.

I specialize in the delicate art of maternity, newborn, and children's photography, where patience, creativity, and a gentle touch are everything. Every session at Dreams Capture Studios is crafted with meticulous attention to detail — from the soft props and custom backdrops to the careful post-processing that gives each image a timeless, gallery-quality finish.
